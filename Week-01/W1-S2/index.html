JESSICA COX

The Armless Achiever

Guiness World Records Title Holder:

  • First licensed armless pilot
  • First armless black-belt in the American Taekwondo Association

Disability Rights Advocate:

  • Established the Rightfooted Foundation International
  • Goodwill Ambassador for Handicap International and made humanitarian trips to Philippines and Ethiopia
  • Motivational Speaker and author of Disarm Your Limits
  • Recipient of US Inspiration Awards for Women 2012: Most Aspirational

Hobby Enthusiast:

  • Certified scuba diver
  • Competed in the 40-mile segment of El Tour de Tucson, a cycling event

As I learned to conquer physical barriers, I developed mental skills that allowed me to go on to achieve the seemingly impossible in my own, unique way. ~Jessica
